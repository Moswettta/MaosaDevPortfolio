About Me

I am a web developer with over four years of professional freelance experience, working with individuals, small businesses, and institutions to build reliable web-based systems that support daily operations.

Throughout my freelance journey, I have designed and developed systems such as school management systems, garage management systems, salon management systems, and portfolio websites. My work typically involves gathering requirements, designing databases, developing backend logic using PHP and MySQL, and creating clean, user-friendly interfaces.

In addition to development, I have provided ongoing system support, updates, bug fixes, and basic hosting assistance for clients. This experience has strengthened my problem-solving skills, time management, and ability to communicate technical ideas clearly to non-technical users.

I enjoy building practical solutions, learning through real-world projects, and continuously improving my skills. I am currently open to opportunities where I can grow further as a developer and contribute to meaningful, impactful projects.
